Featured Recipes

Shawarma

  1. Mix marinade and coat chicken
  2. Make yogurt sauce for topping
  3. Cook chicken on stove or BBQ
  4. Rest chicken for 3 minutes before cutting
  5. Serve on naan wrap and add toppings

Fried plantains

  1. Peel 2 plantains by cutting off both ends
  2. Cut into 1/4 inch thick slices
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of oil to pan and put on medium heat
  4. Add plantain in batches when oil is heated
  5. Fry on each side until golden, about 1.5 minutes
  6. Sprinkle lightly with fine salt *optional

Butter Chicken

  1. Make marinade and marinate for at least 3 hours
  2. Cook chicken and sauce in the same pan
  3. Serve with rice or parathas

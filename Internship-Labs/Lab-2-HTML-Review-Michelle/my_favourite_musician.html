About Dominic Fike

Biography

Dominic Fike is a 28 year old singer, songwriter, and musician. He grew up in Naples, Florida, and has two younger siblings, Alex and Apple. He is black and Filipino. I guess you could also consider him an actor, because he was on a TV show, but it (and he) wasn't very good.

"Florida, bro. It's different."

Musical career

Fike was part of a rap group when he was younger, but none of their songs have been officially released. Since deciding to release music by himself, he went on to release 2 EPs, 2 studio albums, and has been the feature on many other artists' songs.

Other interesting information

Fike was invited to perform at Coachella in 2023.

He also had an acting gig on the show Euphoria, where he played the character Eliott.

He was also arrested and put on house arrest before the release of his first EP, and now uses his platform to raise awareness through The Bail Project.

Discography

Don't Forget About Me, Demos (EP)
His first official release was an EP, which he released while on house arrest (more about that later). It gained so much popularity, that he started working on his first studio album as soon as he could. The EP was titled Don't Forget About Me, Demos.
What Could Possibly Go Wrong
The first studio album was called What Could Possibly Go Wrong, which was released less than a year after the EP. From this, he gained even more popularity and went on to work with bigger musicians such as Justin Bieber and Paul McCartney.
Sunburn
His most recent album was released in 2023, titled Sunburn. While his previous albums were more alternative and featured more heavy rap, this album is considered more of a soft rock vibe.
14 Minutes (EP)
He released another EP without announcing it in 2024, called 14 Minutes. The song Misses has been featured on a few trending charts.

♥ Dominic Fike ♥

Dominic Fike is a 28 year old singer, songwriter and musician from Naples, Florida. And he is my favourite singer.

How I found him

Dominic Fike had been a feature on a Justin Bieber song in 2021, titled "Die For You" and the first time I heard it, I fell in love with his voice and got to googlin' right away.


At this point, he had already established himself as a talented artist, but I had not heard of him. However, not only had he featured on a song with Justin Bieber, but he also did a feature on a song with Paul McCartney. Like, hello?? Sir Paul McCartney? A BEATLE? Talk about established.

What I like about him

Dominic Fike seems like the type of person who really enjoys making music. As someone who loves music, I really appreciate when artists seem to enjoy what they do, rather than just using it as a way to make money. That being said, I think he has gotten so big now that his fanbase is becoming too much for him. He's not into the whole parasocial relationships that come along with a fanbase. Which is totally understandable.
Keeping that in mind, I try not to act like I know him. Cus I don't. But I do think he's fiiiiiiiineeeeee. 😅


Oh, and he went to jail for assaulting a police officer, which I think is kind of funny. And now uses his following to raise money for The Bail Project which covers bail and pre-trial support for those who cannot afford it.

To summarize:

  • He likes music
  • He's fine
  • He hit a police officer........ jk
  • He works with people who have been arrested to offer support for bail and trial

My concert

I got lucky enough to see him live in Baltimore in 2023. I was on a road trip and his concert lined up with my trip (more like the trip lined up with the concert) and it was so fun! Even though I was near the back, I still sang my heart out.

My favourite songs

Honestly, choosing my favourite songs probably feels to some like choosing their favourite child. Like, you know there is one but they won't admit it. But I'll do my best to try.

  1. Bodies
  2. Good Game
  3. Phone Numbers

Music genre

I would call his music kind of alternative. At least in the early days. His most recent album, Sunburn, is maybe more of a soft rock album. But it is also my favourite.

My Favourite Travel Destinations

If you're curious about some of the places I really enjoyed, then you're in the right place! While I haven't been to a lot of places, I have been lucky enough to enjoy some really great places.

Washington, DC

Washington was really cool because all of the museums and the zoo were both free! I was actually only in Washington for a day, so I did half of the zoo and one wing of a museum. I could go there for a week and never be bored.

I highly recommend Washington, DC if you're on a budget!

New York City

While there is a LOT to do in New York, it is certainly not free. Depending on the kind of things you like, you'll never be bored, but may need to spend a little money.

Malibu, Florida

The beach? Impeccable. And free. I don't know if it really matters what beach you go to in Florida, they're all nice. And warm.

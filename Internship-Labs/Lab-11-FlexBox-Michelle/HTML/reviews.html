Sega Genesis: Where it All Began (Reviews)

Revel in Retro interviews real-life users to find out what their favourite games are and what they like about them. Continue below to see more in-depth reviews.

Reviews

Sonic the Hedgehog

By: PnkPrncess22

This game is a classic. It's one of the first video games I ever played, and while they have improved upon graphics as the new versions release, there is something so nostalgic and comforting about playing the original. If I remember correctly, it was also a character that was created by a Sega user in a contest, which is really cool.

Cadillac and Dinosaurs

By: Bl00dFreakN00b

Being able to play on a team was fun, I have fond memories of playing with my brother. When he'd mess up, I would hit him in-game and then in real life.

Disney's Aladdin

By: Nafis101

Aladdin is one of the best animated films and this game is just a fun way to navigate the world that they've made. It's a simple game, with easy to overcome targets but still a lot of fun.
